What is OpenNotification?

OpenNotification is a free service that lets you send instant notifications to websites, apps, or any device. Think of it like sending a text message, but as a notification!

How it works

1

Get your channel

Each notification channel has a unique ID (like a phone number for notifications)

2

Connect your app

Your website or app listens for messages on your channel

3

Send notifications

Send messages instantly to all connected devices

Perfect for

Apps

Send updates to your mobile or web apps

Servers

Send notifications from your servers and backend systems

Smart Homes

Get notifications from your IoT devices
